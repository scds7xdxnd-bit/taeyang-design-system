Tiny Press-Start-2P UPPERCASE pill for status, category, or XP-style micro-labels. Never for body text.

```jsx
<Badge tone="star">Building</Badge>
<Badge tone="mint">Live</Badge>
<Badge tone="coral">In Dev</Badge>
<Badge tone="ghost">★ Specializations</Badge>
```

Tones: `star` (sunshine, default), `sky`, `mint`, `coral` (white text), `purple` (white text), `ghost` (outlined). 10px pixel font, 0.1em tracking — keep labels to a word or two.
